Premium Rooms Package Discription

Our Sea Side Premium Rooms feature available in Bronze, Silver and Gold Themes & either sea views or sea views with balconies. Approximately 30-50 square meters in size, these rooms feature twin,queen and king sized beds and give bright and interesting views due to their elevated location.

Our Sea Side Premium Rooms offer complimentary Wi-Fi, satellite LCD televisions, air conditioning, a safety deposit box, a selection of local teas, complimentary soft drink selection and various luxury amenities. These Rooms enjoy the unrivaled experience of our Long Room, a private lounge with elegant personal service.

Stay in one of our Premier Rooms and enjoy 20% savings at our spa!

All our rooms are non-smoking. Smoking is permitted in designated areas across the property.
