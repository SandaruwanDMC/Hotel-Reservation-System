Sea Side Park Hotel takes pleasure in presenting some of Colombo’s finest cuisine in unique locations throughout the colonial gem.

Sea Side restaurant

Our food offering throughout the day is focused on perfectly executed Ceylonese cuisine, reminiscent of the regional flavors imported by colonial routes, a selection of old and new world wines, along with imported and local beers.

our crab curry is famous island wide, and our scones are the best in Colombo. Our views, from the edge of the Indian Ocean are still spectacular.

In addition to breakfast, lunch and dinner we offer a menu available all day highlighting a gastronomic journey through our island home.

  • Breakfast – 6.30 am to 10.30 am
  • Lunch – 12.30 pm to 2.30 pm
  • High Tea – 4.00 pm to 6.00 pm
  • Dinner – 7.00 pm to 10.30 pm

Sea Kitchen

The most historic landmark in the whole of Colombo, the Chequerboard serves drinks throughout the day. Make sure to get here early to get a seat for the most amazing sun set.

Facing the Sea Side, In... on the green offers casual dining and drinking in a pub atmosphere with live music, sports viewings and quiz nights during the week.

  • Opening time – 4.00pm
  • Closing time – 12.00am

King Pool Bar

The King Pool Bar and Terrace offer all day casual dining in a relaxed outdoor environment next to the swimming pool and overlooking the Indian Ocean.

Sunsets over one of our great cocktails or an ice cool beer are an absolute must as is our famous crab burgers. Enjoy a game of croquet on the South Lawn, adjoining the pool bar!

  • Opening time – 4.00pm
  • Closing time – 12.00am
